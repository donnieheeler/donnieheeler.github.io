Homeland

A crack of the bat.
The smell of the corn,
grows tall in the fields.
You're raising your hand.
against those in need.

"If you don't like it, leave."

I cannot believe,
the words in my ears.
The trumpets and drums.
The wind waving flag.
The sound of the gun.
It deafens my ears,
and oh,
what a beautiful gun.
