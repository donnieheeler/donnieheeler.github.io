Biography

Born March 18, 1996, D.C. Heeler is a multidisciplinary researcher, artist, and technologist. With a foundation in oil painting and digital media, Heeler has expanded his work into the realms of computer programming, literary fiction, and scientific exploration. He is the creator of multiple music albums, an extensive body of artwork, and is currently developing a novel. His interests bridge the gaps between art and science, fostering a philosophy of creative and intellectual synthesis.
