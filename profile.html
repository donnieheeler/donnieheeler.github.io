Biography

Born March 18, 1996, D.C. Heeler is a multidisciplinary researcher, artist, and technologist. With a foundation in oil painting and digital media, he has expanded into computer programming, literary fiction, and scientific exploration. Heeler has created multiple music albums, an extensive body of artwork, and is currently developing a novel. His work bridges the gaps between art and science, fostering a philosophy of creative and intellectual synthesis.

Interests

Art & Design, Literature & Philosophy, Technology & Research, Athletics & Kinetics
